# Privacy Policy / Gizlilik Politikası

**Last updated / Son güncelleme: January 2025**

## English

İzimde respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

### Data We Collect
- **Location Data:** Only collected when you trigger an SOS alert. Not tracked in background.
- **Contacts:** Only the emergency contacts you manually add to the app.
- **SMS:** Used solely to send emergency alerts to your contacts.

### Data We Don't Collect
- No personal information is stored on our servers
- No background location tracking
- No data selling to third parties
- No analytics or advertising data

### Your Control
All data stays on your device. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.

### Contact
For questions: omertass3@gmail.com

- Sunucularımızda kişisel bilgi saklanmaz
- Arka planda konum takibi yapılmaz
- Üçüncü taraflara veri satışı yapılmaz
- Analitik veya reklam verisi toplanmaz

### Kontrolünüz
